Reddit Game Jam — PIETRO

Pietro has some urgent affairs on the right side of the valley! Can you get him there with the least amount of effort possible?

Controls: S bends his knees and F straightens his legs. The relative position of the mouse cursor from his head has a slight effect on the angles at his hip.

If you feel you wasted too much energy in the process, you can press Esc to retry the current level with a clean slate.
